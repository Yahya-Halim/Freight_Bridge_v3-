Our Story

Freight Bridge LLC was founded by Mohamed Soliman — a hands-on operator who believes the owner should be on every job. We're not a big fleet. We're two trucks, one owner, and a commitment to doing the work right.

Cedar Rapids is at the center of Iowa's data center boom. With QTS building a $10 billion campus at Big Cedar Industrial Center, Google investing $576 million nearby, and over 8,500 construction workers on site — the demand for reliable post-construction cleanup has never been higher.

That's where Freight Bridge comes in. We haul out the debris, the decommissioned equipment, and the waste so general contractors can hand off a clean site. We're not building data centers — we're making sure the builders can finish the job.

Mohamed personally supervises every load. He tracks the trucks, manages maintenance, handles insurance, and coordinates with your site manager. When you call Freight Bridge, you're talking to the owner.
