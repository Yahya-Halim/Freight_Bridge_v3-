Our Story

Freight Bridge LLC was founded by Mohamed Soliman — a hands-on operator who believes the owner should be on every job. We're not a big fleet. We're two trucks, one owner, and a commitment to doing the work right.

As Iowa's data center corridor explodes with billions in construction — QTS, Google, Microsoft — there's a massive need for reliable post-construction cleanup. That's where we come in. We haul out the debris, the decommissioned equipment, and the waste so general contractors can move to commissioning.

Mohamed personally supervises every load. He tracks the trucks, manages maintenance, handles insurance, and coordinates with your site manager. When you call Freight Bridge, you're talking to the owner — not a dispatcher, not a call center.
