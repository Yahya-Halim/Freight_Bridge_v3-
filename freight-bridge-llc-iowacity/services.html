Our Services

We haul out what's left after the build is done. Debris, equipment, waste — cleared from your data center site so you can commission.

Construction Debris Removal

Complete post-construction debris hauling from data center build sites. We remove concrete waste, scrap metal, packaging materials, drywall, insulation, and general construction debris. Every load supervised by the owner.

What We Remove

  • ✓ Concrete waste & rubble
  • ✓ Scrap metal & steel
  • ✓ Drywall & insulation
  • ✓ Packaging materials & pallets
  • ✓ Cable spools & wire waste
  • ✓ General construction debris

FAQ

What types of debris do you remove?

We haul concrete waste, scrap metal, wood, drywall, insulation, packaging materials, cable spools, and general construction debris from data center sites.

Can you handle large-volume cleanups?

Yes. We coordinate multi-trip removal schedules for large data center projects and work around your construction timeline.

Do you handle hazardous materials?

We do not handle hazardous materials. We focus on standard construction debris and non-hazardous waste removal.

FAQ

What kind of equipment do you haul?

Server racks, cooling units, UPS systems, PDUs, cable trays, raised floor tiles, and other decommissioned data center infrastructure.

Do you provide data destruction?

We focus on physical removal and transport. We can deliver equipment to your certified ITAD or e-waste recycling partner.

How do you handle heavy equipment?

We coordinate with your on-site rigging team for loading. Mohamed personally supervises every equipment load to ensure safe transport.
